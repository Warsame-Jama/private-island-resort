YOUR PERSONAL SANCTUARY!

At the end of a day filled with adventure and so many fun filled yet tiring activities one would love nothing but to fall onto a bed almost as soft as a bed of roses and reminisce the days activities. We welcome you to pick a room/bed that would suite you from the variety of rooms available at the resort.

The rooms available are:

  • Single Room: A room assigned to one person. May have one or more beds. The room size or area of Single Rooms are generally between 37 m² to 45 m².
  • Double room: A room assigned to two people. May have one or more beds. The room size or area of Double Rooms are generally between 40 m² to 45 m².
  • Triple Room:A room that can accommodate three persons and has been fitted with three twin beds. The room size or area of Triple Rooms are generally between 45 m² to 65 m².
  • Quad Room:A room assigned to four people. May have two or more beds. The room size or area of Quad Rooms are generally between 70 m² to 85 m².
